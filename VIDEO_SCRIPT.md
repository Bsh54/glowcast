# GlowCast — Demo Video Script (Devpost submission)

> **Rules to respect (from the hackathon):** 1–3 minutes, judges stop at 3:00.
> Must explain the YouCam APIs used. Must show the project running on its
> target device (the deployed web app). Public YouTube upload. **No
> copyrighted music, no third-party trademarks** (royalty-free music only —
> e.g. YouTube Audio Library; don't show brand logos on screen).

---

## Preparation (before recording)

1. **Do one full run first** off-camera with the same event/selfie, so every
   screen loads from a warm start and you know the AI outputs. Then reset
   ("Start over") and record a fresh run — timings below assume real waits
   are trimmed in editing.
2. Recording: OBS or Loom, **1920×1080**, browser full screen (F11), 100 %
   zoom, cursor visible. Close other tabs/notifications.
3. Voice: record narration while capturing, or re-record it over the edit
   (cleaner). Speak calmly — the script is ~430 words ≈ 2:50 at normal pace.
4. Event to use on camera: a **wedding 10–14 days away** in a real city —
   it triggers every step (projection + daily-ish plan + weather).
5. Cut every loading wait to ~2 s in editing with a quick crossfade; keep
   the skeletons visible for a moment (they look intentional).

## Timeline & narration (word-for-word)

### 0:00 – 0:15 — The problem (landing page visible, no interaction yet)
**Screen:** the GlowCast landing, slow scroll stop on the hero.
**Say:**
> "You have a big event coming — a wedding, an interview, a gala. And three
> questions hit at once: will my skin be at its best? Which colors actually
> suit me? And how will I really look in the outfit? GlowCast answers all
> three — from a single selfie."

### 0:15 – 0:35 — Step 1: Describe the event
**Screen:** the welcome popup appears → click "OK, got it". Type a real
description in the event field (e.g. *"My best friend's garden wedding,
outdoor ceremony in the afternoon, semi-formal dress code"*), pick the date
(~12 days out), start typing the city and **click a suggestion from the
autocomplete**. Click "Let's go".
**Say:**
> "You describe your event in your own words — no forms, no quizzes. The AI
> reads the dress code and the mood from your sentence. The date sets your
> preparation window, and the city pulls the real weather forecast for the
> day."

### 0:35 – 0:50 — Step 2: The selfie
**Screen:** click "Open camera", face inside the guide, "Capture", then
"Use this photo".
**Say:**
> "One selfie, guided framing. The photo is auto-cropped around the face,
> analyzed in your session, and never stored."

### 0:50 – 1:25 — Step 3: The diagnosis (the wow moment #1 — take your time)
**Screen:** loading messages, then the results. Point the cursor at the
overall score, then **click one weak gauge** → the mask appears showing the
detected zones → read its one-line insight. Then scroll to the color
palette.
**Say:**
> "YouCam's Skin Analysis scores eight concerns from the image — hydration,
> radiance, texture, redness and more — plus an overall score. Tap any
> score and you see exactly *where* it was detected on your face, with a
> concrete tip grounded in dermatology guidance. Then, YouCam's Facial
> Color Tones extracts your exact skin, eye, lip and hair colors — and the
> AI derives your color season and a personal palette of shades that
> flatter you."

### 1:25 – 1:50 — Step 4: Skin projection
**Screen:** the before/after appears → **drag the divider line** slowly
across the face, twice.
**Say:**
> "This is your skin's potential — generated from your own photo, targeting
> your three weakest scores. Not a promise: a direction. The full
> day-by-day skincare plan that gets you there is waiting in the final
> summary."

### 1:50 – 2:25 — Step 5: The four looks (wow moment #2)
**Screen:** the fitting-photo card → take/upload the upper-body photo →
four skeletons → the four looks appear one by one. Hover each label.
**Say:**
> "Now the stylist dresses you. The AI reads your event, your palette and
> the weather forecast, shops a 250-outfit catalog, and YouCam's Clothes
> Virtual Try-On renders four directions **on your own body** — a classic,
> a statement, a relaxed take, and a wildcard. Four real previews of
> yourself, before you buy anything."

### 2:25 – 2:45 — Step 6: Summary + PDF
**Screen:** the summary page — scroll through looks, palette with hex
codes, the skincare timeline. Click **"Download my plan (PDF)"** and show
the downloaded PDF for 2 seconds.
**Say:**
> "Everything lands in one plan: your four looks, your palette with hex
> codes to show in-store, and your dermatology-grounded skincare timeline —
> downloadable as a PDF you take shopping."

### 2:45 – 3:00 — The close (retail value + APIs recap)
**Screen:** back on the summary, or a simple end card (see below).
**Say:**
> "Under the hood: four YouCam APIs — Skin Analysis, Facial Color Tones,
> Clothes Virtual Try-On, and the AI Image Generator — orchestrated by an
> AI agent. For a retailer, this is a conversion engine: try-ons are proven
> to cut returns by up to forty percent. GlowCast — arrive at your big day
> with zero doubt."
